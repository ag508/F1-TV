Step 1: Download the IBO App
The IBO Player app is available on multiple platforms, including Android, iOS, and Firestick.

For Android & Firestick:
Open the Google Play Store (or Downloader app on Firestick).

Search for “IBO Player” and install it.

If the app isn’t available, download the APK file from a trusted source and sideload it.

For iOS (iPhone/iPad):
Visit the App Store and search for “IBO Player”.

Download and install the app.

If unavailable, you may need to use TestFlight or a third-party app store.

Step 2: Launch the IBO App
Once installed, open the app. You’ll see a simple interface with options like “Playlist”, “Settings”, and “EPG”.

Step 3: Add Your IPTV Subscription
To use IPTV on IBO Player, you need an M3U URL or Xtream Codes API from your IPTV provider.

Open the app and go to Settings.
Select “Add Playlist” and enter your M3U URL or Xtream Codes details (username, password, and server URL).
Click “Save” and wait for the channels to load.
